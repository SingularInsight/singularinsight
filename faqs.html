FAQs & Business Information

How do payments work?

We use secure third-party payment links (e.g., Stripe). You can pay by card, ACH, or wallet where enabled. For projects over $400, we take 50% upfront and 50% at delivery.

Do you require logins or accounts?

No. There is no login or signup for our site. We’ll share files via email or a secure link.

What about taxes?

For Maryland customers, certain IT/data services may be subject to MD service tax and digital downloads may be subject to MD digital product tax.

Data privacy (PII/PHI/PCI)?

Please avoid uploading sensitive data. We provide a “Data Intake Red Flags” checklist and delete working copies within 14 days of delivery unless ongoing support is requested.

Refunds & reschedules

Mini Triage ($99) is non-refundable but credited if you start a package within 7 days. One free reschedule with 24 hours notice.
